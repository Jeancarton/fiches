PIPPI LONGSTOCKING (Fifi Brindacier)

Astrid Lindgren, 1945

Résumé

Pippi lives alone with her horse and monkey. She does whatever she wants, is always kind, without any filter in the way she acts and speaks. She does not know the rules of politeness in society, so she makes many mistakes, which in the end come to be funny. Her two neighbours, Tommy and Anika, are fond of her and learn a lot from her way of living and seeing things.
